## Corrections policy

Accuracy is the foundation of our credibility. When we get something wrong, we fix it promptly and transparently.

**How to report an error:** Email [editor@kratomnewstoday.com](mailto:editor@kratomnewstoday.com) with the briefing URL and the specific error. We investigate all reports.

**How we handle corrections:** When a factual error is confirmed, we update the briefing with a correction note at the top, clearly stating what was wrong and what is correct. The original text is not silently altered. The correction note includes the date of the correction.

**What counts as a correction:** Factual errors (wrong dates, wrong names, misattributed quotes, incorrect regulatory details). Typos and minor grammatical fixes are made silently. Substantive changes to meaning or fact always get a correction note.
