## Editorial mission

Kratom News Today exists to provide daily, source-attributed coverage of the kratom industry across regulation, business, and science. Our editorial standard is straightforward: report what is being reported, attribute every claim, and link to primary sources.

## Editorial process

Our daily briefings are produced using an AI-assisted editorial workflow governed by explicit editorial rules. Every briefing passes through research, synthesis, voice check, rules check, and compliance check stages before publication. The editorial rules that govern this process are designed to produce coverage that meets traditional journalistic standards.

For more on our process, see the [About page](/about/).

## Editorial independence

Kratom News Today is published by Herba Releaf. Editorial coverage is independent of the publisher's commercial operations. Specifically:

- Editorial article bodies never link to commercial pages, including Herba Releaf's properties
- Herba Releaf is covered like any other industry company
- Advertising is clearly labeled and structurally separated from editorial content
- The publisher relationship is disclosed on every page of the site

## Sourcing standards

Every factual claim in our coverage is attributed to a named source. We prioritize:

- Primary regulatory documents (FDA, DEA, state government sites, court filings)
- Peer-reviewed research (DOI links, journal pages)
- Established journalism outlets
- Trade press original reporting
- Industry advocacy organization announcements (when newsworthy)

We do not link to commercial pages, product listings, or affiliate URLs in editorial content.

## Conflicts of interest

Our publisher, Herba Releaf, is a kratom industry participant. This creates an inherent conflict of interest that we manage through structural separation of editorial and commercial operations, prominent disclosure, and consistent editorial standards applied equally to all industry participants, including our publisher.
