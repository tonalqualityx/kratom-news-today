Kratom News Today ("KNT," "we," "us") is published by Herba Releaf, based at 281 West 6th Street, Wyoming, PA 18644. This policy describes what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

## What we collect

### Website analytics

We use **Google Analytics** (via Google Tag Manager) to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects:

- Pages viewed and time on page
- Referring URLs and search terms
- Device type, browser, operating system, and screen resolution
- Approximate geographic location (city/region level, derived from IP address)
- A randomly assigned client identifier stored in a cookie

Google Analytics does not collect your name, email address, or other personally identifying information through its standard implementation. Google's use of this data is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

We use **Google Tag Manager** to manage analytics and tracking scripts on the site. GTM itself does not collect personal data, but it loads scripts (such as Google Analytics) that do.

### Cloudflare

Our site is served through **Cloudflare's CDN** (content delivery network). Cloudflare processes connection data (IP addresses, request headers) to deliver pages and protect against abuse. This is standard CDN functionality, not a separate analytics product. Cloudflare's handling of this data is governed by [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

### Email subscriptions

When you subscribe to our daily briefing, we collect your **email address** through a form powered by **Klaviyo**. Your email address is stored in Klaviyo's systems and used to send you the Kratom News Today daily briefing.

Your KNT subscription is maintained on a **separate mailing list** from any other Herba Releaf communications. Subscribing to the KNT daily briefing does not subscribe you to Herba Releaf product announcements, promotions, or marketing emails. These lists are kept separate.

Klaviyo's handling of subscriber data is governed by [Klaviyo's Privacy Policy](https://www.klaviyo.com/legal/privacy-notice).

You can unsubscribe from the daily briefing at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

### Advertising

KNT runs **direct-sold advertising** from Herba Releaf and potentially other sponsors. These are static placements served from our own infrastructure. We do not use programmatic ad networks, real-time bidding, or third-party ad tracking pixels.

Sponsor advertisements are clearly labeled as "SPONSORED" and structurally separated from editorial content.

## Cookies

We use the following cookies:

| Cookie | Set by | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|--------|---------|----------|
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique visitors | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique visitors | 24 hours |
| `__cf_bm` | Cloudflare | Bot detection | 30 minutes |

We do not use cookies for advertising targeting or cross-site tracking.

## How we use your data

- **Analytics data** is used to understand readership patterns, identify which coverage resonates, and improve the site. We do not sell analytics data or use it for advertising targeting.
- **Email addresses** are used to deliver the daily briefing you subscribed to. We do not sell, rent, or share subscriber email addresses with third parties. We do not use your KNT subscription to send unrelated marketing.
- **Cloudflare data** is used to deliver the site and protect against attacks. We do not access individual-level Cloudflare logs.

## Third-party services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

- **Google Analytics / Google Tag Manager** (Google LLC)
- **Klaviyo** (Klaviyo, Inc.) for email
- **Cloudflare** (Cloudflare, Inc.) for CDN and security
- **Cloudways / DigitalOcean** for hosting

Each service processes data according to its own privacy policy, linked above where applicable.

## Your rights

### California residents (CCPA)

You have the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used. You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. You have the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

### EU/EEA residents (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You have the right to restrict or object to processing. You have the right to data portability. Our lawful basis for processing analytics data is legitimate interest (understanding readership). Our lawful basis for processing email subscriptions is consent (you opted in).

### All visitors

To exercise any of these rights or to ask questions about this policy, contact us at [editor@kratomnewstoday.com](mailto:editor@kratomnewstoday.com).

## Children

KNT is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.
